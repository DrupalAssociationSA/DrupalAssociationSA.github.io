Become a Member

Contact us at info@dasa.org.za with the membership you are interested in.

We'll send you details for an EFT payment.

  • If the membership is for a company (Gold, Silver or Bronze), include a link to your company' profile on Drupal.org .
  • If the membership is for an individual, include a link to your profile on Drupal.org .
  • If the membership is for a website (Diamond Site Owner or Graphite Site Owner), include a link to the website.

If you cannot find these links, e-mail us at info@dasa.org.za and we will help you.

We link only to Drupal.org individual and company profiles and only list (no hot link) the domains of site ownership unless they have a Drupal.org link too.
