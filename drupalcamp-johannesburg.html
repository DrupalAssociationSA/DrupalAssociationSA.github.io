Renate Ehlers

Renate started web design and -development in 2014 following a career in the hospitality industry. She got introduced to Drupal when her company wanted to expand their services offered to include responsive design and Content Management Systems. She joined the user group and regularly attends meet-ups. She has been elected to serve on the DASA board as Vice Chairman for the 2014/2015 term.

SurpoNet

SuproNet was established in 2001 to provide a one stop service with respect to the design, development, hosting, maintenance, update and marketing of websites for smaller organisations and to help them to utilise their web presence as best as possible. Although we did not actively market our services as web designers before 2001 (when SuproNet was established), we did develop a few sites on request. We have been developing websites since 1997 and have grown with the use of the Internet in South Africa. SuproNet is a division of SUPROKON Management Services that was founded in 1990 and has been involved in the IT industry since 1994.

Keynote

Greg will be delivering the Keynote for the Drupal Camp in Johannesburg, to welcome the community and share a vision of the future based on DASA's mission.

Greg McKeen

Greg McKeen has been an active member of the Drupal Johannesburg community since 2007. Out of high school, he first worked with Burtronix and later joined Telamenta as a Drupal Developer, Programmer and Project Manager. Since 2012, he has been the Managing Director of Telamenta. In 2013, the Drupal Association South Africa was established, and Greg was elected to be the Boards first Chairman. In 2014, he was voted onto the Board by the Public, and accepted the role of Secretary.

Telamenta

Telamenta is an Open Source Web and Mobile Applications Development Agency based in Johannesburg and Cape Town, South Africa. Our primary goal is to build systems that make a difference. In developing partnerships with our clients, we are able to focus on the strategic value of project development in respect to the overall vision and mission of their businesses. We've developed systems which manage language translation with an ambitious goal of capturing "Every word, in every language" with kamusi.org. Online application systems, including the registration of accounts, membership management, CPD (continuous professional development) tracking, e-commerce platforms, and mobile applications for municipalities and staff.
