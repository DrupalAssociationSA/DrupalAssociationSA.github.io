BUILDING A COMMUNITY

Drupal Association South Africa (DASA) is a non-profit organisation established for the public benefit objective to foster and support the Drupal project, the community and its growth in South Africa.

We aim to help the South African Drupal community by hosting regular meetups where knowledge on Drupal and supporting technologies is shared. The meetups also act as a support platform for anyone using Drupal, from novices to experts. We also help the community with funding, infrastructure and education.

These initiatives are made possible with funds that come from memberships, supporting partners, donations, and volunteers.

If you like to join us, here is how you can get invloved:

  • Sign up for our meetups
  • Share on a topic that you are passionate about during one of the meetups
  • Participate in our Agora project
